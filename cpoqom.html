Colonial Poqom

This page provides resources about Colonial Poqomchi' and Poqomam, collectively "Colonial Poqom."

Transcriptions

The following are raw transcriptions that I have made of handwritten Colonial Poqom documents. These are public domain, so feel free to use these at your discretion, though I would appreciate being credited if you copy large portions of a transcription. If you are using one of these documents for research, it may be a good idea to check it against the original.
  • Pedro Morán's Arte breve of Poqomam from 1720 (last updated March 22, 2021). Original in the Bibliothèque Nationale de France, Manuscrits américains, Item #53; this version based on a photocopy in the William Gates papers at BYU.
