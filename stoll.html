Stoll 1888, Die Maya-Sprache der Pokom-Gruppe

This page contains a partial English translation of Otto Stoll's (1888) grammar of Poqom, originally written in German. Stoll's work is excellent; in my opinion, the quality of his description compares favorably to many grammars of Mayan languages from the mid-20th century. It is therefore valuable not just for identifying historical changes over the last 130 years (there are surprisingly few), but as a description in its own right.

This translation was done primarily through Google Translate, with manual edits by me based on my understanding of the context and my limited knowledge of German. Because the purpose at this point is simply to have a text readable by English speakers, the translation is not polished and would benefit from revision by a more proficient speaker of German. Any mistakes are my own.
